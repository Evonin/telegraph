Sick of Written Letters?

Fear no more with the Telegraph!

This new system was created by Samuel Morse and it uses electricity to create signals, which travel between long distances.

These pulses of electricity travel through a series of copper wires that are hung on the side of roads, which travel to your destined telegraph.

Who Made This?

The telegraph was made by inventor, Samuel Morse. He is a professor of art at New York University and likes to invent new things for solving different problems. Before this he was a painter and always wanted to paint paintings at the US capital but hasn't been picked to attend.
